Optimization Case Study

Production Mix Profit Optimization

A linear programming model for Weigelt Corporation that determines how many large, medium, and small air conditioners to produce at three plants while respecting capacity, storage, demand, and utilization constraints.

GitHub Evidence

Business Question

How should production be allocated across plants to maximize profit under operating constraints?

Result Signal

The R/lpSolve model identifies an optimal profit of about $707,400 and highlights Plant 2 storage as a binding constraint.

Model Structure

  • Decision variables: product units by plant and product size.
  • Objective: maximize unit profit across large, medium, and small products.
  • Constraints: plant capacity, storage limits, product demand, and common capacity utilization.
  • Tooling: R, lpSolve, knitr, kableExtra.
