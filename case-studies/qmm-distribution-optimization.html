Optimization Case Study

Production & Shipping Cost Optimization

A transportation linear programming model that selects a lowest-cost production and shipping plan from plants to wholesalers while satisfying demand and capacity limits.

GitHub Evidence

Business Question

How should production and shipping be planned to minimize total cost?

Decision Value

Provides a cost-minimizing allocation plan that satisfies wholesaler demand while balancing plant capacity.

Model Structure

  • Decision variables: units shipped from each plant to each wholesaler.
  • Objective: minimize production plus shipping cost for all routes.
  • Constraints: plant supply capacity and wholesaler demand fulfillment.
  • Tooling: R, lpSolve, knitr.
